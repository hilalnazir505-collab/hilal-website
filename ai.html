📘 Artificial Intelligence

🔹 What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

🔹 Types of AI

  • Narrow AI: Works for specific tasks
  • General AI: Human-like intelligence
  • Super AI: Beyond human intelligence

🔹 AI vs Human Intelligence

  • AI is fast in calculations
  • Humans are better at creativity
  • AI works on data, humans use emotions

🔹 Key Terms

  • Algorithm: Step-by-step solution to a problem
  • Dataset: Collection of data
  • Model: Trained system that makes predictions

🔹 Real-Life Examples

  • Google Search
  • Netflix recommendations
  • Face recognition
